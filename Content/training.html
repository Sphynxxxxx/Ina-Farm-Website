Our Training Programs

At Ina Farmers Learning Site & Agri-Farm Inc., we are committed to providing high-quality agricultural education and training to farmers, aspiring agriculturists, and community members. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to equip participants with practical skills, technical knowledge, and innovative approaches to sustainable farming.

Through our partnerships with government agencies and agricultural institutions, we offer accredited courses that lead to nationally recognized certifications, enhancing the employability and productivity of our graduates.
