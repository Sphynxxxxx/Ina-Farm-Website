GET TO KNOW US

Ina Farmers Learning Site and Agri-Farm Inc., located in Brgy. Agcuyawan Calsada, Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo, is an accredited Learning Site for Agriculture (LSA) by the Agricultural Training Institute (ATI). It is also recognized as as an Agri-Tourism Farm Site by the Department of Tourism (DOT). The farm serves as an avenue for training, seminars, workshops, and demonstrations conducted by different implementing agencies, companies or departments as well as, the programs initiated by the farm. Moreover, as a learning site, we centered in progressing the operation of agricultural machinery, as well as serves as educational platforms, engaging visitors and community members about agriculture, farm mechanization, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

A premier institute for farm mechanization and Agri-tourism in Western Visayas.

Our Mission

To serve as a prime institute for post-harvest mechanization and advancement in agriculture and associated industries, as well as to promote Agri-tourism in Western Visayas.
