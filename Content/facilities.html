Casa Ely Verde

Our dishes are crafted from the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients, many of which are cultivated on our very own agri-farm. From hearty, rustic meals to light, garden-fresh options, every bite celebrates sustainability, flavor, and ELY’S enduring legacy.

Demo Area

The demo area at Ina Farm showcases a variety of crops grown using sustainable and innovative farming techniques. It serves as an educational hub where visitors can observe organic farming, intercropping, and crop rotation in action. Efficient irrigation systems and natural soil enrichment methods, such as composting and mulching, promote sustainability. The area also demonstrates eco-friendly pest control, providing a hands-on learning experience for farmers and visitors interested in modern and traditional agricultural practices.

  • Sustainable Farming
  • Harvesting equipment
  • Showcases Various Crops
