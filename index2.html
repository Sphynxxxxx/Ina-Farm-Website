Ina Farmers Learning Site and Agri-Farm Inc., located in Brgy. Agcuyawan Calsada, Barotac Nuevo, Iloilo, is an accredited Learning Site for Agriculture (LSA) by the Agricultural Training Institute (ATI). It is also recognized as an Agri-Tourism Farm Site by the Department of Tourism (DOT). The farm serves as an avenue for training, seminars, workshops, and demonstrations conducted by different implementing agencies, companies or departments as well as, the programs initiated by the farm.
