Trainings & Seminars

Empowering farmers with knowledge and skills for sustainable agriculture

Our Training Programs

At Ina Farmers Learning Site & Agri-Farm Inc., we are committed to providing high-quality agricultural education and training to farmers, aspiring agriculturists, and community members. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to equip participants with practical skills, technical knowledge, and innovative approaches to sustainable farming.

Through our partnerships with government agencies and agricultural institutions, we offer accredited courses that lead to nationally recognized certifications, enhancing the employability and productivity of our graduates.

Accreditation

Our facility is recognized by key agricultural and educational institutions

Agricultural Training Institute (ATI)

Certified as a Learning Site for Agriculture by the Agricultural Training Institute (ATI), the training arm of the Department of Agriculture, providing quality hands-on agricultural training and extension services to our community.

Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA)

Accredited training center by TESDA, offering nationally certified agricultural courses including:

  • Farmers Field School - Organic Agriculture Production NC II
  • Farmers Field School - Rice Machinery Operations NC II

Training Programs

Comprehensive agricultural training programs for all skill levels

Organic Agriculture Production NC II

A comprehensive course on organic farming practices, covering soil management, natural pest control, composting, organic fertilizer production, and sustainable crop production.

Duration: 3 weeks Offered quarterly

Rice Machinery Operations NC II

Technical training on operating and maintaining rice production machinery including land preparation equipment, transplanting machines, and harvesting equipment.

Duration: 4 weeks Offered bi-annually

Sustainable Water Management

Training on efficient irrigation systems, water conservation techniques, rainwater harvesting, and sustainable water management practices for agriculture.

Duration: 1 week Monthly workshops

Value-Added Product Development

Hands-on training on processing agricultural products to increase their market value, including food preservation, packaging, and basic entrepreneurship skills.

Duration: 2 weeks Quarterly courses
